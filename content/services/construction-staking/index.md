## Construction Staking Services

Construction staking involves transferring design plans from drawings to the field to ensure improvements are built in the correct location, elevation, and alignment. Accurate staking reduces costly errors and supports project efficiency.

### Typical Services

- Building layout and offsets
- Foundation and footing staking
- Grading and curb staking
- Utility alignment and elevation staking
- As-built data collection

### Benefits

- Improves construction accuracy
- Reduces rework and delays
- Provides clear field reference points
- Supports quality control and verification
